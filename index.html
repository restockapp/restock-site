What it does

📦

Smart pantry tracking

Quantities, units, categories, low-stock alerts — and a beautiful list/card view of every item in your kitchen.

📷

AI photo recognition

Snap a milk carton, a tomato, or a wine bottle — Apple Intelligence identifies it and fills in the details.

🍳

What can I cook?

Tap once. Foundation Models suggests three recipes from what's already in your pantry.

💬

Talk to your pantry

"I bought 2 dozen eggs." "What's running low?" A chat assistant with tool calling.

🌷

Gift tracking

Flowers wilt, cake goes stale, wine has a drink-by. Track gifts with countdown rings and photo timelines.

🛒

Smart shopping list

Auto-generated from low-stock items, grouped by supermarket aisle so you shop in one pass.

100% on-device.
